About Driftwood Lane

Crafted by hand, with a gentle Cornish feel.

Tucked away in the heart of Cornwall, Driftwood Lane is a small, whimsical world of soft textures, gentle colours, and timeless charm.

Each piece is lovingly handcrafted using the traditional art of needle felting, transforming natural wool into unique keepsakes and miniature works of art.

From woodland scenes and seasonal decorations to bespoke creations, every item is made with care, character, and a quiet sense of wonder.

Bespoke requests welcome

Looking for a different colourway or a more personal piece? Send a message with your idea and I’ll do everything I can to create something just for you.
